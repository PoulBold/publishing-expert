QR codes are everywhere — on posters, packaging, menus, business cards, and product labels. But not all QR codes are equal. If you want to track who scans your code, from where, when, and how often, you need more than just a static image. You need a free QR code generator with tracking — and free QR code generator offers exactly that.

What Makes a QR Code Trackable?

A trackable QR code doesn’t just point to a URL. It routes users through a redirect link that logs scan data before forwarding them to the final destination. This allows you to collect essential analytics without changing the original URL.

PuzzleGenerator’s free QR generator automatically assigns each code a redirect ID. Every scan gets recorded — including timestamp, IP, location (if available), device type, and frequency. You can view this data anytime from your dashboard. No paid account, no subscription — tracking is included by default.

Who Needs QR Code Tracking?

Small businesses use trackable QR codes to monitor customer behavior on flyers, coupons, or storefronts. Authors and creators add them to books or merchandise to measure reader engagement. Event organizers use QR codes for RSVP tracking or live feedback during sessions. Restaurants measure menu traffic from physical tables. E-commerce stores link to landing pages or discount offers, adjusting marketing strategy based on scan data.

Whether you’re printing thousands of flyers or embedding a code in a digital PDF, scan analytics help you understand what works — and what doesn’t.

How the Puzzle Generator QR System Works

You can return anytime and review performance. Scan statistics are displayed in a clean format, showing total scans, unique users, and activity over time. Each QR code remains editable. That means you can change the target URL anytime, even after printing — a huge advantage over static QR systems.

This flexibility is critical in time-sensitive campaigns, where links may need updating or redirecting without reprinting materials.
